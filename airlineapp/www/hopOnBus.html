SIA Hop on Bus

The SIA Hop-On touring system offers visitors an easy way to enjoy Singapore sights. Covering 4 bus routes (red, yellow, blue and brown line) with strategically located stops across the city, it is an efficient way of touring Singapore at your own pace. More than a tour, it also doubles up as a transportation system. With a 24-hour validity, you get to start your tours at your own time. It is now easier to see more of Singapore for less. Do look out for the City Sightseeing red front livery before you board the bus.
