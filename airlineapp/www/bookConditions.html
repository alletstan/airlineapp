Booking Eligibility

  • A Basic Singapore Stopover Holiday package (hotel + transfers) and Singapore Explorer Pass may be purchased by passengers transiting through Singapore, en route to their final destination (for example, Sydney – Singapore – London), and by passengers visiting Singapore as a mono destination (for example, Sydney – Singapore - Sydney).

  • A Singapore Stopover Holiday package (hotel + transfers + attractions) may only be purchased by passengers transiting through Singapore, en route to their final destination (for example, Sydney – Singapore – London).

  • Singapore Stopover Holiday packages and Singapore Explorer Pass options are available to the following:
    • Passenger travelling on Singapore Airlines (SQ) and/or SilkAir (MI) ticketed and operated flights into and out of Singapore. The flight number must begin with SQ or MI. Any 4-digit SQ flight must be operated by SilkAir, and any 4-digit MI flight must be operated by Singapore Airlines.

    • Passenger travelling on itineraries consisting of Singapore Airlines (SQ) and/or SilkAir (MI) ticketed and operated flights connecting to Scoot (TR) operated flights.

  • A Singapore Stopover Holiday package (hotel + transfers + attractions) may only be purchased by passengers transiting through Singapore, en route to their final destination (for example, Sydney – Singapore – London).

  • You will receive an electronic SSH receipt when you purchase a Singapore Stopover Holiday package. This document will contain the name of the confirmed hotel and information on transfers.

  • The Basic Singapore Stopover Holiday (“BSSH”), Singapore Stopover Holiday (“SSH”) and Singapore Explorer Pass ("SGEP") are only available to passengers travelling on Singapore Airlines Limited ("SIA") and SilkAir (Singapore) Private Limited ("SLK") flights, except: Unaccompanied passengers below 18 years of age Passengers travelling on agent, interline or industry discounted travel, and/or free air tickets
